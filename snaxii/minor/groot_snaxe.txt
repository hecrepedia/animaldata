[History]
<p>The Groot Snaxe is a Snaxe that was spied by eagle eyed
 (intentional) viewers of the film Guardians of the Hecretary.
 For a split second during the credits, one frame showing the
 Groot Snaxe in all its glory was shown. Using the Indian Movie
 Pirates (IMP) technique, the frame was hunted down and captured,
 and all of the current information of the Groot Snaxe comes
 from that one frame.</p>

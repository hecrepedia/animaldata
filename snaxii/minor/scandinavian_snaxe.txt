[Bio]
<p>They are pure Scandinavian. Found in Scandinavia, the
 birthplace of the first Primitive Snaxe. They are some of
 the biggest, fastest, strongest, and smartest Snaxii around
 (With a few exceptions), and their home is in deep Snaxe
 territory. If you see one, run.</p>
[Personality]
<p>These bad boys train in the mountain, and like to get buff.
 They also do Scandinavian things, whatever they do. For example,
 they like keeping the streets clean. If you see clean streets,
 be on guard.</p>
[History]
<p>Yes, they are pretty ancient.</p>
[Fun Facts]
<p>The weakest ones are only 50x stronger than a cobra.</p>
